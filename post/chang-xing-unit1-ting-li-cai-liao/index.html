一、听力

3

4

5

(三)听长对话，选择最佳答案。你将听到三段对话，请根据对话内容，选出每个问题的最佳答案。每段对话读两遍。

请听第一段对话，回答第11至13小题。

Great! I spent it with my grandpa in a small town.

What did you do there?

I went fishing with him and we learnt a lot about nature. That was a surprising holiday.

(四)听短文，选择最佳答案。你将听到一篇短文，请根据短文内容，选出每个问题的最佳答案。短文读两遍。

My name is Gary. Before I was twelve, my family lived in the mountains. Our house was far from my school, and it took me one hour to walk to school every morning. It was a happy time for me because I could meet friends on the way and we would go to school together. There were about ninety students in my school. We spent most of our time playing. The mountains were our playground. I still remember different kinds of trees and flowers in different seasons. Everything was so interesting and beautiful. Even now, I still miss my life in the mountains.
