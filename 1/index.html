一、听力

(一)听句子，选图片。你将听到五个句子，请选出与所听句子内容相符的图片，有一幅图是多余的。每个句子读两遍。

1

The clouds are clean and soft, like our white sheep.

2

3

4

5

He lives in Fujian. His hometown is famous for tea.

No. I like the panda best.

8

Of course. The rainforest is the lung of the earth.

9

About three hundred.

10

(三)听长对话，选择最佳答案。你将听到三段对话，请根据对话内容，选出每个问题的最佳答案。每段对话读两遍。

请听第一段对话，回答第11至13小题。

Hi, George! I read a report about wild animals in a magazine yesterday.

What does the report say, Maria?

It says many wild animals are in danger.

Is the magazine's name Nature?

Yes, it is. After I read it, I felt very angry. Some people killed elephants for their ivories.

They are too cruel. In my opinion, people must stop killing animals for money.

I agree with you. People should know “No trading, no killing”.

Really? Where did you plant the trees?

Did you walk there or take the bus?

How many trees did you plant?
